SNAP benefits

COLORADO PEAK


You can apply for SNAP benefits, (food stamps), on the Colorado PEAK website, or fill out an application and turn it in at their office. However, currently due to covid-19 they are short staffed, and you are likely to have a quicker result by applying online.

SSI/SSDI

If you live in Colorado and would like to apply for Social Security disability, you have a few options. You can apply for disability in person by visiting your local Social Security office. You can also call the Social Security Administrationâ€™s customer service number at 800-772-1213. A few notes, however, the social security office is shorthanded due to covid-19, and some weeks will be closed to the public all together.


Before applying you will need to have a mailing address. If you are staying in a shelter, then talk to them about getting your mail there. If you are camping or on the streets, then apply for a PO Box at a post office center. They have programs for homeless populations.


You will also need to get a medical doctor or psychiatrist to fill out and sign a Med-9 form in order to qualify for SSI/SSDI. You can get this done at Stout Street Health Center.


Finally, if you are denied SSI/SSDI, then you need to appeal the decision. You can do this on your own, however, it is best to use a lawyer. They have to give you resources for this upon your request. Once you start the appeal process you will start getting AND payments to your EBT card. This money is a stop gap measure so you do not have to go without while the appeal process drags out.
