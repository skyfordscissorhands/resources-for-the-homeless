Colorado Village Collaborative

Colorado Village Collaborative next to Denver Community Church. The Colorado Village offers a safe camp site designed by engineers without borders. This site is also staffed with social workers and counselors, and a mobile shower station. Check out their website as they also offer tiny home villages.
